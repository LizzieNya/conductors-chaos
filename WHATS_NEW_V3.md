# 🎮 What's New in v3.0 - The Gamification Update

## Major New Features

### 🎯 WarioWare-Style Mini-Games
The biggest addition! Quick, frantic mini-games that appear during concerts:

1. **🎵 Rhythm Match** - Memorize and repeat a 4-note pattern
2. **🎶 Note Catcher** - Catch falling notes with your basket
3. **👆 Tempo Tap** - Tap on the beat for perfect timing
4. **🎩 Conductor Says** - Simon Says with conducting commands

- Spawn randomly during gameplay (30% chance every 20-30s)
- 6-12 second duration for quick bursts of fun
- Bonus score and XP rewards
- Full-screen immersive experience

### 📊 Complete Gamification System

**XP & Leveling**
- Earn XP from concerts and mini-games
- 1000 XP per level
- Unlimited progression
- Visual XP bar with progress tracking

**Player Titles**
- 7 unique titles based on level
- From "Novice Conductor" to "Immortal Maestro"
- Displayed on player card

**Daily Challenges**
- New challenge every day
- 6 different challenge types
- Rewards scale with player level
- Real-time progress tracking
- Instant coin rewards on completion

**Streak System**
- Build daily play streaks
- Track longest streak
- Visual fire emoji indicator
- Encourages consistent play

**Player Statistics**
- Total plays
- Lifetime score
- Perfect concerts
- Current & longest streaks
- Last play date

### 🎨 New UI Components

**Gamification HUD**
- Always visible in top-right corner
- Shows level, XP progress, and title
- Displays current streak
- Shows daily challenge progress
- Completion indicators

**Mini-Game Overlays**
- Full-screen immersive experience
- Clear instructions
- Timer displays
- Score feedback
- Success/failure animations
